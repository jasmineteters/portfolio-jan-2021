About Me

My name is Jasmine Teters, I just turned 30, which was not exciting. I'm from Portland, Oregon. I moved to Utah to play for the Utah Falconz, women's semi pro women's tackle football team. I play offensive line as well as serve on the board of directors and lead the Recruiting Committee.


I enjoy riding bikes, whether it's a road bike or a mountain bike, I like being on it! My favorite place to ride is up Emigration Canyon, which is about 7.6 miles to the top, with 1200+ ft elevation gain! It's a challenge, but I love it every single time!


I got into coding because my sales career has not been super stable during Covid, and I've always had a knack for systems and puzzles, so I figured that this would be a good outlet for that talent. I can't wait to see what I'm capable of!
